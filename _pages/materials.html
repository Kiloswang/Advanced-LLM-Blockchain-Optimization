--- layout: archive title: "Studying Materials" permalink: /materials/ author_profile: true --- There are some materials for you to learn more about LLM and Blockchain Basics: * Large Language Models: * Stanford CS 336: (https://stanford-cs336.github.io/spring2025/), a good course for you to understand and implement an LLM from scratch. * 大语言模型: (https://github.com/datawhalechina/llmbook) An introductory course from Renmin University. * TTIC 31230: Fundamentals of Deep Learning (https://mcallester.github.io/ttic-31230/). If you are interested in deep learning from a theoretical side. * Blockchain: * Principles of Blockchains: (https://web3.princeton.edu/principles-of-blockchains/) Covers most topics you should know about blockchain.
